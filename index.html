Our Mission

Everyone Deserves a Second Chance

2nd Chance Ministries exists to bring renewal and restoration through faith in Jesus Christ and practical support for life’s toughest challenges. We are dedicated to helping individuals and families overcome obstacles such as addiction, homelessness, domestic violence, and unemployment. Through outreach events, Bible studies, and one-on-one support, our mission is simple: to show God’s love by giving people the tools and the hope they need for a second chance.
